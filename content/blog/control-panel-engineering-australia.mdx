## What to review before release

| Area | What to look for |
| --- | --- |
| Compliance | AS/NZS 61439 and AS/NZS 3000 addressed in the design package |
| Documentation | Schematics, terminals, cable schedules, BOM, and revision control |
| FAT | Clear point checks, power-up sequence, and defect closeout |

## What this means

A well-prepared panel package usually saves time in the workshop and again during commissioning. It also leaves the site with documentation it can actually use.
