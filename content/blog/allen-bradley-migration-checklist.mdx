Allen-Bradley migration projects should focus on scope validation, I/O verification, and a staged commissioning plan that protects uptime.

## Validate hardware lifecycle

Confirm spare strategy, firmware path, and compatibility before procurement.

## Build a migration test plan

Prepare simulation, FAT scripts, SAT scripts, and rollback process.

## Execute staged commissioning

Commission by functional area and keep clear defect tracking until stable production is achieved.
